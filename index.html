My Works

Medical Consultation App

This is a modern, user-friendly medical consultation web app designed to simplify healthcare access. It allows patients to seamlessly connect with licensed doctors for virtual consultations, manage prescriptions, and receive real-time medical support — all from the comfort of their homes. The platform is secure, intuitive, and tailored for both patients and healthcare providers, making it easier than ever to access quality care anytime, anywhere.

Digital Mobile Banking for Opay

For this project, I designed a modern and user-friendly mobile banking experience for OPay, a digital banking app aimed at simplifying financial transactions for everyday users. The core objective was to create an intuitive interface that offers seamless access to essential banking features such as money transfers, bill payments, airtime top-ups, and wallet management. I focused on a clean layout with bold visual hierarchy, ensuring that users could navigate easily without feeling overwhelmed.
